Aaron Schwen — Founder, CharterSelect

CharterSelect is the only insurance agency built exclusively for charter schools. Founded in 2011 by Aaron Schwen, we've spent over a decade building deep expertise in charter school risk — property, liability, employee benefits, and the regulatory landscape that makes charter schools different from every other education institution.

Most brokers treat charter schools like small businesses or school districts. We don't. We built our agency around charter schools because we believe that specialization leads to better outcomes — better coverage, better pricing, and a broker who actually understands your school's mission.
